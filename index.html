ELIGIBILITY

Who can join TheGreatEnglishWarriors?

Find the right entry scheme for your level and goals. We welcome learners who are serious about improving their English.

Foundation Patrol

Ideal for beginners with little or no English foundation.

  • Age: 16 - 25
  • Basic schooling completed
  • Motivation to learn daily

Communication Command

For learners with basic knowledge who want to speak with confidence.

  • Age: 17 - 28
  • Completed school-level English
  • Ready for live speaking drills

Leadership Language

For advanced learners preparing for professional and academic success.

  • Age: 18 - 30
  • Strong English foundation
  • Ambitious career goals
