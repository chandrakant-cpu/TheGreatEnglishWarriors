What You'll Master

This 12-week intermediate program takes your English skills to the next level. You'll learn complex grammar structures, expand your vocabulary, and develop the confidence to communicate in professional and social settings.

Month 1: Complex Grammar

  • Perfect tenses (have/has been)
  • Modal verbs (can, could, may, might)
  • Conditionals (if clauses)
  • Passive voice

Month 2: Professional Communication

  • Business vocabulary and idioms
  • Email and report writing
  • Presentations and meetings
  • Negotiation skills

Month 3: Advanced Speaking

  • Debate and discussion techniques
  • Storytelling and anecdotes
  • Expressing opinions and arguments
  • Handling difficult conversations
