What You'll Learn

This intensive 8-week program is designed for complete beginners who want to build a strong foundation in English. Through structured missions and daily practice, you'll learn essential grammar, vocabulary, and pronunciation skills.

Week 1-2: Basic Grammar & Vocabulary

  • Present simple tense
  • Basic greetings and introductions
  • Numbers, days, and months
  • Family and personal information

Week 3-4: Everyday Communication

  • Present continuous tense
  • Shopping and food vocabulary
  • Asking for directions
  • Describing people and places

Week 5-6: Past Tenses

  • Past simple tense
  • Irregular verbs
  • Telling stories
  • Daily routines in past

Week 7-8: Future Plans & Review

  • Future tenses (will, going to)
  • Making plans and arrangements
  • Comprehensive review
  • Final assessment
